# Marketing Core — Last 30 Days

## Inputs
- Spend, leads, CPL, ROAS by channel
- Top campaigns (winners/losers)
- Major experiments and outcomes

## Output format
- What improved
- What regressed
- What was learned
- What to stop / continue / scale

## Decision policy
- Scale only what has measurable signal.
- Stop weak experiments quickly.
- Preserve a clear test backlog for next cycle.
